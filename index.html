About HelpR

Back

There are many situations we face outdoor where we need help from our surroundings like to get a jump start your car,push a wheelchair, lift, shelter, first aid, changing tire, removing snow or a tree that has fallen on the pathway you are driving; but we either do not know who is around us or needs us. So, our team from their personal experience and after intervening and surveying a lot of people decided to resolve this issue by our HELPING application for mobile devices.


This application is designed to ask for help and help others via notification within a given radius.
